TMR
Team Manipal Racing is the official off-road racing club of Manipal University. We design and fabricate All-Terrain Vehicles(ATVs) according to the rules laid by SAE BAJA. The three words “INNOVATE. INITIATE. INCARNATE” shape the work at Team Manipal Racing. We not only conceptualize ideas but also substantiate them into reality. TMR took up its first project at the SAE BAJA which was held in Wisconsin, USA. It was the first Indian team to qualify for the competition and one of the two teams representing Asia. The team stood at 14th position in design. After an overwhelming and successful first year, the team participated in SAE BAJA Rochester 2010. They were one of the 4 Asian teams to qualify. The team had to design and fabricate an amphibious vehicle. They finished at 62nd position which was the best finish by an Indian team ever. In 2012, the team participated in SAE BAJA Wisconsin. They were the 1st Indian team to qualify the technical inspection and scored 287/1000, which was the highest scored by an Asian team. They were the Best Indian Team for the 3rd consecutive year. In 2013, the team made its maiden performance at SAE BAJA India. They were awarded “2nd Safest Vehicle” award. In SAE BAJA India 2014, the V4 clocked 6.66 seconds making it the “4th fastest car” at the event. By implementing the concepts practically and competing at BAJA events worldwide, we are not only building off road cars but also creating real engineers and making the university and the nation proud.
Manipal University

Manipal University is synonymous with excellence in higher education. Over 28,000 students from 57 different nations live, learn and play in the sprawling University town, nestled on a plateau in Karnataka’s Udupi district. It also has nearly 2500 faculty and almost 10000 other support and service staff, who cater to the various professional institutions in health sciences, engineering, management, communication and humanities which dot the Wi-Fi-enabled campus. With off campuses in Dubai, Mangalore, Bangalore, Jaipur, Melaka (Malaysia), the University provides opportunity for aspiring students to gain technical and medical education through world class facilities which are constantly reviewed and upgraded to reflect the latest trends and developments in higher education. Manipal Institute of Technology

The Manipal Institute of Technology is a constituent institution of the Manipal University that specializes in the fields of engineering and technology. MIT contains 16 academic departments and awards undergraduate, graduate and doctoral degrees. Established in 1957 in Manipal, Karnataka, India, MIT is one of the first self-financed colleges in India. Over the years, the institute has made a place amongst the most premier engineering colleges in country. Learning starts when the theoretical knowledge is practically implemented and MIT specializes in the field of providing practical knowledge to the students with many equipped workshops and student projects.
